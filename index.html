The Motivation

When a rare bird is spotted, it sends a ripple through the digital community. This project investigates how "citizen scientists" react to rare sightings across the Danish landscape.

The Sunday Spike

Our analysis of the DOFbasen dataset reveals clear human patterns. Birds might migrate by the stars, but birders migrate by the weekend calendar.
